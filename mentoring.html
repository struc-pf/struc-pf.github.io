Data Mentoring

In today's data-driven world, data literacy is becoming increasingly vital as businesses face an abundance of data. It plays a pivotal role in helping organisations navigate the complexities of the digital landscape, transforming data challenges into opportunities for informed decision-making while mitigating potential risks associated with handling data.

However, embracing new technologies and skills can be intimidating, even for those who consider themselves relatively technical. One of the challenges we've observed is that many online resources assume a certain level of prior technical knowledge, which can be discouraging for beginners. We understand that this can hinder the learning process, and we strive to make our sessions more accessible to learners at all levels.

At our service, we understand the importance of personalised learning, which is why we offer flexible one-to-one mentoring or advisory sessions tailored to individuals' technical backgrounds and learning needs. Whether you have limited data experience, are looking to enter the data world or switch careers, or are a business owner seeking a better understanding of your data, our sessions are designed to meet your specific goals. We also cater to those seeking guidance in shaping problem statements and finding the right resources. Contact us at info@struc.co.uk for more information or rates.
