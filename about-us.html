About Us

At Struc, we understand the struggles of scattered data, unclear metrics, and uncertainty about where to start. Many organisations, even big corporations, face these challenges. Despite abundant online resources, taking that first step remains tough. Moreover, many consultancy solutions assume a certain level of existing data infrastructure and specific skills to maintain the solution effectively. We acknowledge the significant gap between the fast-moving tech world and the day-to-day operations of SMEs and charities. We aim to bridge this gap and support you in tackling open questions and achieving your data goals.

Working closely with your leadership and operational teams, we derive insightful conclusions that inform crucial decisions. Our expertise lies in establishing new input and reporting systems, drive growth through data analysis, and refining metrics to match your team's skills. Beyond improvements, we optimise existing systems and data processes while providing essential training materials.

We welcome collaborations with IT companies, aiming to enhance data capacity through strategic partnerships. We specialise in translating business requirements into technical specifications, ensuring seamless alignment and driving transformative results.
